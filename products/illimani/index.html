illimani touch tap

Be in control of? The new touch tap is dynamic simple to use and looks great

Chilled water dispense

Touch the blue cold button once and it will automatically dispense for up to 50 seconds or until you touch the blue button again. If you keep your finger on the button for more than 1 second it will dispense conventionally. When you touch the blue button the dynamic temperature of the cold water shows on the LED display.

Safety button

Touching the safety button the hot button flashes for 3 seconds. Touch the hot flashing button for instant boiling water.

Boiling water dispense

Touching the safety button the hot button flashes for 3 seconds. Touch the hot flashing button once and it will automatically dispense for up to 50 seconds or until you touch the red button again. If you keep your finger on the button for more than 1 second it will dispense conventionally. When you touch the red button the dynamic temperature of the boiling water shows on the LED display.

Have you ever tasted pure water?

Water constitutes around two thirds of the human body. It plays a vital part in our breathing, in transporting nutrients and taking away waste; it helps keep our body the right temperature and allows us to absorb vitamins from our food. We lose water simply by going about our day to day activities yet many of the fluids we drink, such as coffee and tea, are diuretics – in other words they cause the body to lose water. There is no argument that water is essential to life, but does the quality of the water we drink really matter?

Filtered Water does not constitute Pure Water, it filters out a small amount of organic chemicals so it improves the taste slightly. Purified Water is exactly what it says it is… Purified, free of lime scale, chemicals, dissolved metals, pharmaceutical drug traces, cryptosporidium and any other substances prevalent in the water supply today. It gives you peace of mind and increases the unit’s life-cycle considerably.

In The words of Dr Robert Verkerk the Executive Director of the Alliance of Natural Health “Why would you choose anything other than Purified Water for your colleagues and family? the water is guaranteed pure, tastes crisp and refreshing and is economical to use”

Water test

Pure water does not conduct electricity, the dissolved solids in the water conduct electricity. If the water is pure and the dissolved solids removed there can be no reaction when a electrical current is passed through the water.
