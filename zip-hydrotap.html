Dr. Robert Verkerk

Executive Director of Alliance for Natural Health Endorses illimani boiling and Chilled tap.

Pure water technology has been around for some years and over the past 10 years its use in domestic systems has become increasingly available. However, these systems have been out of reach of most households due to the expense of their production. Now illimani boiling and chilled tap has come up with a system using this superb technology which is easily available to all. Easy to install, the units do away with the plastic water bottle, the disposal of which is an environmental problem, not to mention the labour intensive job of lugging bottles home from the supermarket. Jug filters have been popular in the past but illimani boiling and chilled tap system takes drinking clean pure water into a new level. Their units enable households to drink pure, fresh, clean, healthy water everyday. Many illnesses in our society are preventable and through eating healthily and drinking pure water, the incidences of cancer and heart disease can be dramatically reduced. Drinking the guideline recommended number of 8 glasses a day makes more sense when people drink water from the illimani boiling and chilled tap system.
